## Language policy (강제)
- UI text = English (CEFR Pre-A1, 5-7yr old vocabulary)
- Character dialogue = English
- Korean = ONLY for learning targets (자모, 단어, 문장)
- Romanization = always show alongside Korean
- English meaning = always show alongside Korean (그림 또는 번역)

## Examples
✅ "Tap the letter that says 'ga' (가)"
✅ "What does 사과 (sa-gwa) mean? Apple? Banana? Orange?"
❌ "ㄱ을 터치하세요" (UI in Korean)
❌ "사과는 빨간 과일이에요" (lesson in Korean)

## Vocabulary scaffolding
- New Korean words always with: visual + pronunciation + English
- Repeat new word 3+ times in one Quest (spaced)
- Cumulative: words from earlier Episodes appear naturally
